My Favorite Flowers

Celosia Dragon's Breath

Celosia are annual's that love the sun. They are very colorful and can come in shades of red, pink, orange, purple, white, and yellow. Celosia can attract butterflies. Click Here

Morning Glory

Morning glories are flowers that love the sun and bloom during Summer and Fall. These flowers come in 5 main colors blue, pink, purple, red, and white. These colors can also be mixed, such as a white flower with 5 purple streaks coming from the center. Morning glories tend to attract birds and butterflies. Click Here

Brain Celosia

Brain celosia are also known as Cockscomb or Wool flowers. These flowers bloom from late summer through late fall and are annuals. The main colors for these brain like flowers are red and yellow. Click Here

Hybrid Tea Rose

Hybrid tea roses are typically used in bouquets because of their wide range in color and their long stems. They are the oldest of the modern rose types and have high disease resistance. Click Here

Dahlias

Dahlias have 30 species and 20,000 cultivars, giving a huge range to choose from. There are dwarf dahlias that grow no taller than 12 inches and some that can grow taller than a man. The width of dahlias can grow between 1 inch and being as wide as a dinner plate. Click Here
